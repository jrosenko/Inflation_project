visualization, exploritory analysis, and possibly prediction. Create pipelines to visualize and predict.

Pieces of the pipelines:

sources and saves it as an output file for later use downstream in pipeline.

certain inflation data in desired ways

3) predication scripts or function - no one can do this well, except maybe the next month
or so.  perhaps scripts to do this either for the short term (next month or so) or
